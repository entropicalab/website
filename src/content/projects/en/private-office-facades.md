brief: |
  the developer had a structural shell already designed and a façade procurement
  decision still open. the brief was a paid simulation study to compare nine
  reasonable envelope variants, three shading depths against three glazing
  specifications, and recommend one for construction.

  no architecture was changed. only the wall.
approach: |
  the model ran on the building's actual orientation and adjacency, with three
  weather files (tmy, hot extreme, recent year). each of the nine variants got
  a cooling load, a daylight autonomy reading, and an indicative capex delta.

  the recommendation was the middle shading depth with the better glazing, not
  the most expensive option on either axis. the developer signed it; the building
  was built.
strategies:

  - label: orientation tuning

  - label: roof spec
