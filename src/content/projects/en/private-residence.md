brief: |
  colón is on the caribbean side of the isthmus, hotter, wetter, and with a softer breeze
  than the pacific side. the client asked for a house that did not need a grid connection
  to be comfortable, not a house that *looked* sustainable.

  the brief is honest, which is why the project takes its time. the model is asked
  questions weekly and the section moves with the answers.
approach: |
  the design opens with three rooms tested against the same site: orientation,
  ventilation, and roof. the orientation set the long axis east-west to keep the
  short faces facing the sun. the ventilation study set the apertures by the
  prevailing northeast trade. the roof became a single overhanging plane,
  shading the south of the house and carrying the photovoltaic array on its
  upper face.

  this is a project in process. the numbers will firm as the building is built.
strategies:
